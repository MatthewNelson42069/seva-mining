<objective>
Make `sub_quotes` independent of the cross-agent dedup gate by passing
`dedup_scope="same_type"` into `run_text_story_cycle(...)`. This is the last
remaining delta between the current 7-sub-agent topology and the user's stated
spec (per CONTEXT.md D-05 handoff note in of3's SUMMARY): quotes should be able
to draft a quote-format piece from an article that breaking_news / threads /
long_form already drafted today, mirroring how `sub_infographics` was made
independent in of3.

Purpose: Stop losing the best quotes of the day. Currently, if `sub_breaking_news`
(cron 08:00) drafts a strong Reuters/Bloomberg story at the morning tick, the
cross-agent dedup predicate in `_is_already_covered_today` will block
`sub_quotes` (cron staggered later) from drafting a pull-quote variant of that
same article — even though the content_type, visual artifact, and use case are
completely different. Quotes should behave like infographics, gold_media, and
gold_history: independent pipelines that each produce their best output of the
day.

Output: One kwarg added to `quotes.py::run_draft_cycle`, one updated docstring
mirroring infographics.py's D-02 reference style, and one new sibling test in
`test_quotes.py` mirroring `test_run_draft_cycle_passes_new_kwargs`.
</objective>
