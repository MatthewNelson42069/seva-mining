<objective>
Make the Breaking News sub-agent (`sub_breaking_news`) run on a 1-hour cadence (was 2h) while keeping the other 6 sub-agents on 2h, AND fix a latent APScheduler edge case where offset=0 jobs skip their first fire on every scheduler boot (which forces a full-interval dark period after every Railway redeploy).

Purpose: Operator wants breaking news drafts surfaced roughly twice as often as the other content types (time-critical) without disrupting the staggered 2h cadence for the other 6 sub-agents. The offset=0 first-fire skip is a latent bug exposed by the Breaking News row sitting at offset=0 — the +10s buffer ensures `start_date > scheduler.start()` wall clock so IntervalTrigger doesn't treat the first fire as "missed."

Output:
- Per-agent `interval_hours` field on each `CONTENT_SUB_AGENTS` row (extend tuple from 5 → 6 fields)
- `sub_breaking_news` row gets `interval_hours=1`; other 6 rows keep `interval_hours=2`
- `+timedelta(seconds=10)` buffer on `start_date` in `build_scheduler`
- Explanatory comment above the buffer line
- Refreshed docstrings/comments across 5 lines in worker.py + 1 line in breaking_news.py
- Single atomic commit on branch `quick/260421-m9k-breaking-news-hourly`
</objective>
