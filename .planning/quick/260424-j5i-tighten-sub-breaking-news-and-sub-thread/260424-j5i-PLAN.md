<objective>
Tighten story selection for `sub_breaking_news` and `sub_threads` so each run only surfaces "top of top" gold + critical-minerals coverage. Composite-score sort + top-N cap + minimum-score floor, plus two folded-in user directives (remove `rare earth restrictions` keyword; threads cadence 4h -> 3h) and one recency-curve refinement (<48h=0.3 bucket).

**Why this matters:** the per-run WhatsApp firehose shipped in i8b currently approves every story that clears the binary Haiku gate + compliance. Selection quality is happening at the gate layer, not at the selection layer. This task wires `breaking_news` and `threads` to the battle-tested `max_count + sort_by="score"` pattern used by `infographics.py:359-364`, adds a `min_score=6.5` floor, and adds telemetry.

**Applies ONLY to:** breaking_news + threads. Four other sub-agents (quotes, infographics, gold_media, gold_history) are out of scope.

Output: deterministic diffs across `scheduler/agents/content/{__init__,breaking_news,threads}.py`, `scheduler/agents/content_agent.py`, `scheduler/worker.py`, and five test files; SUMMARY.md capturing the 41% breaking_news retention observation as post-launch observability signal.
</objective>
