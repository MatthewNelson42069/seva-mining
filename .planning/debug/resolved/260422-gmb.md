## Resolution
<!-- OVERWRITE as understanding evolves -->

root_cause: Two compounding bugs in _search_gold_media_clips in gold_media.py introduced by vxg (68b21d1):
  BUG 1 (dominant): Query requires `gold` as keyword. Only 3 of 7 accounts post native videos
    (BloombergTV, ReutersBiz, FT), and none include the literal word "gold" in video tweet text —
    they post macro/economic commentary clips. Account reorder in vxg removed gold-sector
    accounts (Kitco/BarrickGold etc. that did say "gold") and added broad financial media that
    don't. Result: zero matches on every run since vxg landed.
  BUG 2 (compounding): GOLD_MEDIA_ACCOUNTS[:5] slice excluded FT (position 5) — one of the 3
    accounts that actually posts videos. FT was unreachable even if gold keyword had been fixed.

fix: scheduler/agents/content/gold_media.py — three changes:
  1. Changed accounts_clause from GOLD_MEDIA_ACCOUNTS[:5] to GOLD_MEDIA_ACCOUNTS (all 7)
  2. Changed query from "({clause}) has:videos gold -is:retweet" to "({clause}) has:videos -is:retweet"
     (removed `gold` keyword — LLM quality bar in _draft_gold_media_caption evaluates gold
     relevance more precisely via 3-criteria gate: identifiable analyst, substantive gold
     commentary, gold focus)
  3. Expanded run_draft_cycle notes telemetry:
     - Zero-return path: {x_returned, passed_video_filter, llm_accepted} (added in prior session)
     - Success path: {x_candidates, llm_accepted, compliance_blocked, queued} (added this session)
